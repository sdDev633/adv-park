FAQ

COMMON
QUESTIONS

Can I get a refund if I can't make it?
+

Yes. Cancel up to 48 hours before your visit for a full refund. Within 48 hours, we offer a free date change. No-shows are non-refundable.

Is there a discount for large groups?
+

Groups of 15+ get 20% off, and groups of 30+ get 30% off. Contact us via the Contact page for customised group packages.

What is the minimum height for rides?
+

Most family rides have no height restriction. Thrill rides require 130cm+, and extreme rides require 140–150cm. All restrictions are displayed at each ride entrance.

Can I upgrade my ticket on the day?
+

Yes! You can upgrade your Explorer pass to All-Day at any ticketing counter inside the park by paying the difference.

Is the park open on public holidays?
+

We are open 365 days a year, including all public holidays. In fact, holidays are some of our biggest event days — we often run special programming!
