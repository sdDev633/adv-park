How to Get Here
By Car: Take NH65 toward Shamshabad. We are 5 mins from RGIA Airport. Free parking for 5,000 cars.

By Metro: Nearest metro is Mindspace (Red Line). Park shuttle every 30 mins from 9AM.

By Bus: TSRTC routes 291K, 218M stop directly at the park gate.
